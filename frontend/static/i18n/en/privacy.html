Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025-01-10

At Burning Message, we prioritize your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Data Collection

We do not store user-generated content (messages or images).

No user accounts or personally identifiable information (PII) are required to use the platform.

Temporary metadata (e.g., IP addresses) may be processed solely for service functionality and is discarded after a short period.

2. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies or tracking mechanisms to monitor user behavior.

3. Data Security

Content is encrypted during transmission.

Despite these measures, we cannot guarantee absolute security. Users share content at their own risk.

4. Third-Party Access

We do not share user data with third parties unless required by law (e.g., to comply with legal requests).

5. Your Rights

You have the right to understand how your data is handled and to discontinue use of the service if you disagree with these terms.

For questions or concerns, contact us via contact info on GitHub information page.
